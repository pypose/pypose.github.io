Key Supporting Labs

SAIR Lab

State University of New York at Buffalo

Our research goal is to achieve human-level spatial awareness and reasoning on robotic systems.

ARI Lab

Pennsylvania State University

Our mission is to advance state-of-the-art aerial autonomy by bridging the aerospace and robotic communities.

AIR Lab

Carnegie Mellon University

Our mission is to advance state-of-the-art autonomy.

RSL

ETH Zürich

The Robotic Systems Lab (RSL) designs machines, creates actuation principles and builds up control technologies for autonomous operation in challenging environments.

SPARK Lab

Massachusetts Institute of Technology

The SPARK Lab works at the cutting edge of robotics and autonomous systems research for air, space, and ground applications.
